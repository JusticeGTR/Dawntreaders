Dawntreaders

We are a Final Fantasy XIV free company on Brynhildr focused on having fun and helping others in the community.

About

Dawntreaders, previously known as Heavensward, was formed on March 30th, 2015. We are a rank 30 Free Company that is aligned with the Order of the Twin Adder, and are aiming to foster a healthy and helpful community on Brynhildr.

We welcome and appreciate all of our LGBTQIA+ members. We aim to always respect each other's preferred pronouns, and encourage folks to correct others using an incorrect pronoun. We do not tolerate the use of racial slurs, misogyny, homophobia, transphobia, or any other form of derogatory speech.

Weekly Meeting

We have a party every Sunday at 6 p.m. PT/9 p.m. ET. We spend a bit of time discussing upcoming patch notes/in-game updates, community initiatives, and promotions. The most important part is when we give away some awesome items!

While friends outside the FC are welcome to join for the fun, only FC members may participate in rolling for raffle items.

Rules
